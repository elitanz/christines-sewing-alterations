Northeast Minneapolis

Eighteen years of making gowns fit like they were made for you.

Christine has been quietly transforming wedding dresses, bridesmaid gowns, and formal wear from her studio on Wilson Street NE since 2008 — and in that time she's personally altered over 400 wedding dresses. No rushing, no assembly lines. Just one exceptionally skilled seamstress, one customer at a time.

She listens to exactly what you need — whether it's taking in a bodice, shortening a lace hem without disrupting the pattern, adding custom sleeves, or creating a wrist hold instead of a bustle — then does the work by hand until the fit is right. Her prices are fair and upfront. Her 5.0-star rating on over 83 reviews says the rest.
